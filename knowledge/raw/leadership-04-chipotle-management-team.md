# Management

The information provided below about Chipotle is intended to provide additional information for our investor community.  Chipotle does not warrant that this information is full or complete and investors should rely on their own resources for complete research.

[Scott Boatwright](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Executive Officer

**Scott Boatwright** is the Chief Executive Officer and a member of the Board of Directors for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in November 2024, Mr. Boatwright previously served as Interim Chief Executive Officer since August 2024. Prior to that, he held the role of Chief Operating Officer/Chief Restaurant Officer since May 2017. Mr. Boatwright was named "Operations CREATOR of the Year" by Nation's Restaurant News in 2021 and named to Fast Casual's "2020 Top 25 Executives" list.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Boatwright spent 18 years with Arby's Restaurant Group in various leadership positions. He most recently served as the Senior Vice President of Operations, where he was responsible for the performance of over 1,700 Arby's restaurants in numerous states.

Mr. Boatwright is a member of Business Roundtable, Business Council, and National Restaurant Association's Fast Casual Industry Council. He holds a Master of Business Administration from the J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University.

[Adam Rymer](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Financial Officer

**Adam Rymer** is the Chief Financial Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, responsible for corporate and field financial planning and analysis, as well as the financial reporting, tax, treasury and investor relations functions. Mr. Rymer is a member of the Executive Leadership Team, reporting to the Chief Executive Officer. He previously served as Vice President of Finance and has held a variety of positions in Finance and People Experience since joining the organization in 2009.  Additionally, he is the Treasurer of the Chipotle Cultivate Foundation. In 2025, Mr. Rymer was named Extel All America Best CFO for the restaurant sector.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Rymer held corporate finance positions at Rock Bottom Restaurants and Travelocity and was also a Which Wich franchisee.

Mr. Rymer has a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in finance from the University of Texas at Austin.

[Curt Garner](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer

**Curt Garner** is the President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Prior to this, he served as the Chief Customer and Technology Officer for nearly a decade since joining the organization in November 2015. Mr. Garner has been instrumental in developing Chipotle's digital platform and the integration of technology across the organization as well as ensuring data security. His leadership has secured global recognition for Chipotle's platforms including the award-winning Chipotle app and the brand's loyalty program, Chipotle Rewards. In 2020, Mr. Garner was named to the CIO Hall of Fame, and in 2025, he was featured on Forbes's CIO Next list, recognizing the top 50 leaders using tech for business impact.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Garner worked at Starbucks Corporation for 17 years, most recently serving as Executive Vice President and Chief Technology Officer. He led the brand's global technology and engineering services and was responsible for creating and building the Starbucks app, loyalty program, website and mobile payment. Before that, Mr. Garner was responsible for leading the development and deployment of technology for Wendy's international division as well as the technology that supported Tim Hortons initial expansion into the U.S. marketplace.

Mr. Garner has a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics from The Ohio State University.

[Laurie Schalow](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer

**Laurie Schalow** is the Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. In her role, Ms. Schalow oversees all internal and external communications, stakeholder engagement, sustainability, customer service, social listening, government affairs, food safety and quality assurance for the brand's 4,000+ restaurants in the U.S., Canada, and Europe.  Additionally, Ms. Schalow is responsible for oversight of The Chipotle Cultivate Foundation, where she serves as President.

Recognized for her work and dedication to furthering the communications industry, Ms. Schalow has been named to Nation's Restaurant News's Power List (2025), Business Insider's 'Most Influential PR Pros' list (2021, 2020), Fast Casual's Top Movers and Shakers list (2021), PRNews' Top Women in PR list (2021), and received PR Week's "Most Purposeful In-House Communicator" Award (2020).

Prior to joining Chipotle in 2017, Ms. Schalow was Vice President of Public Affairs for Yum! Brands, overseeing global corporate social responsibility, public relations, crisis management, social listening and community diversity programs for the 44,000 KFC, Pizza Hut and Taco Bell restaurants in 140 countries.

Ms. Schalow holds a Bachelor of Science degree in business from Miami University and received her Master of Business Administration from Case Western Reserve and Wayne State University. She currently serves on the Board of Directors for the National Restaurant Association where she chairs the Advocacy Strategy Committee. Ms. Schalow is also an advisory board member for Wine Country Women as well as a member of the Fast Company Impact Council, Forbes Communications Council and the International Women's Forum.

[Ilene Eskenazi](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer

**Ilene Eskenazi** is the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill.  Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Eskenazi previously served as the Chief Human Resources Officer for two years at the organization.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Eskenazi served as the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Petco Health and Wellness Company, where she oversaw compensation and benefits, human resources operations, talent management, as well as all legal matters. Before moving to Petco, Ms. Eskenazi was the Global General Counsel and Chief Human Resources Officer at Boardriders, Inc. (previously Quiksilver, Inc.). She also held the post of Chief Legal Officer and Senior Vice President of Talent Operations and Performance at True Religion Apparel, Inc. as well as senior leadership positions at Red Bull North America, Inc. and The Wonderful Company.

Ms. Eskenazi serves on the Board of Directors of a.k.a. Brands Holding Corp. She holds a Juris Doctorate from the University of California, Los Angeles' School of Law as well as a Bachelor of Arts degree in philosophy from the University of Michigan.

[Jason Kidd](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Operating Officer

**Jason Kidd** is the Chief Operating Officer at Chipotle. He joined the organization in 2025 and is responsible for overseeing operations of the company's 4,000+ restaurants as well as development for its new restaurant openings.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Kidd served as the Global Chief Operating Officer at Taco Bell. Before joining Taco Bell, he held the role of President of Hearing Lab Technology, LLC. Mr. Kidd spent six years leading operations at 99 Cents Only Stores, most recently serving as President and Chief Operating Officer. Additionally, he held roles of increasing responsibility across operations, merchandising, planning and supply chain at Sam's Club for 20 years. Mr. Kidd currently serves on Share our Strength's No Kid Hungry Dine Advisory Board.

Mr. Kidd holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the Sam M. Walton College of Business at the University of Arkansas.

[Kerry Bridges](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Food Safety Officer

**Kerry Bridges** is the Chief Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Bridges previously served as Vice President of Food Safety overseeing food safety standards, quality assurance and procedures.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Bridges oversaw supplier food safety for the world's largest food retailer, Wal-Mart, serving over 200 million customers around the world on a weekly basis. During her time at Wal-Mart, Kerry's food safety oversight included tens of thousands of Wal Mart's, Neighborhood Market's, and Sam's Clubs food suppliers, in addition to researching and assessing new and emerging food safety issues and handling regulatory compliance. Prior to Wal-Mart, Ms. Bridges food safety career included roles with TESCO and Jack in the Box, Inc.

Ms. Bridges is the past president of the International Association for the Southern California Affiliate of Food Protection (IAFP) and former board member of the Global Food Safety Initiative (GSFI).  She attended California Polytechnic State University, where she received a bachelor's degree in food science.

[STEPHANIE PERDUE](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Senior Vice President of Brand Marketing

**Stephanie Perdue** is the Senior Vice President of Brand Marketing, overseeing brand strategy, advertising, innovation, experiential, social media and brand activation. An expert marketer with over 20 years of experience in the restaurant industry, Ms. Perdue is recognized as a catalyst for insight-driven strategy, category-leading innovation and disruptive integrated marketing.

Ms. Perdue's career experience spans both the restaurant and entertainment industries. Prior to Chipotle, Ms. Perdue served as the Chief Marketing Officer at TGI Fridays, where she led brand strategy and planning, development and execution of marketing, advertising, digital/off premise and culinary. She also held senior leadership roles at Taco Bell for over 12 years, ultimately serving as the Chief Product Marketing Officer, responsible for driving marketing strategy and innovation, including leading the teams that designed Doritos Locos Tacos, Taco Bell breakfast and the value menu.

Ms. Perdue's accomplishments include generating record sales and profit for Fortune 500 companies, receiving Adweek's Advertising Women of New York "Changing the Game" Award, being inducted into Ad Age's 2020 Class of Women to Watch, and being featured as one of Fast Company's Most Creative People in Business in 2016. Stephanie holds an MBA from the UCLA Anderson School of Management and a business economics undergraduate degree from UCLA.

[NATE LAWTON](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Chief Business Development Officer

**Nate Lawton** is the Chief Business Development Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing international business development opportunities, emerging concepts and partnership strategy.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Mr. Lawton spent over 20 years at Procter & Gamble working across Fabric Care, Beauty Care, Personal Health Care and Food & Beverage. [Katherine Laird](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Senior Vice President, Chief Supply Chain Officer, Chipotle

**Katherine Laird** is the Senior Vice President, Chief Supply Chain Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Since joining the company in 2018, she has held leadership roles across procurement, distribution and logistics, bringing an end-to-end perspective to the company's supply chain operations.

Laird supports Chipotle's Food with Integrity mission and leads the strategy to build a global supply chain focused on quality, transparency and long-term sustainability. She has more than 20 years of experience across foodservice, consumer goods and retail, with expertise in logistics, sourcing, network optimization and change management.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Laird held supply chain roles at Owens-Illinois, where she led inventory planning, network optimization, and digital transformation including SAP, DRP and TMS across North American operations.

Laird holds a Master of Business Administration from Michigan State University and a Bachelor of Business Administration from Siena Heights University. She also completed the Stanford Executive Leadership Program.

[Nevielle Panthaky](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Senior Vice President of Culinary

**Nevielle Panthaky** is the Senior Vice President of Culinary at Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing culinary strategy, menu innovation, product commercialization and cross-functional collaboration with supply chain, marketing and operations. Mr. Panthaky, a classically trained chef who joined the organization in 2018, is recognized for insight-driven menu creation, category-leading innovation and scalable execution rooted in real ingredients and classical techniques. He guides teams of chefs and cross-functional experts from initial validation through pilots and national launches and leads experimentation at the Chipotle Cultivate Center, an innovation kitchen at UCI Research Park in Irvine, California.

Mr. Panthaky's career spans hotels, fine dining and leading national restaurant brands. Raised by a hotelier and immersed in hospitality, Mr. Panthaky moved to the United States over two decades ago. He graduated from The Culinary Institute of America in 2003 and began his career in New York's fine-dining restaurants. Subsequent roles included culinary leadership at P.F. Chang's, Panda Restaurant Group and Dine Brands.

Mr. Panthaky holds an MBA from the University of California, Irvine's Paul Merage School of Business.

[Michael (MJ) Johnston](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

**Michael Johnston** is the Vice President of Finance at Chipotle Mexican Grill, leading the company's global Finance organization in support of its mission to Cultivate a Better World. [Garrett Calderwood](https://ir.chipotle.com/management)

Vice President of Operations Services

**Garrett Calderwood** is the Vice President of Operations Services at Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing the strategies, tools and support that enable operational excellence, consistency and scalability across the company's restaurants. A results-driven operator with deep field experience, Mr. Calderwood focuses on simplifying execution, strengthening team capabilities and elevating the guest and crew member experience. Mr. Calderwood joined Chipotle over 11 years ago, most recently serving as Regional Vice President, where he was responsible for over 450 restaurants across the Northeast U.S. He led seven Directors, 60 Multi-Unit Leaders and all regional support staff across seven Chipotle sub-regions covering eight states.

Before joining Chipotle, Mr. Calderwood was a Field Consultant for 7-Eleven and began his career as a Regional Account Manager at Bloomberg.
