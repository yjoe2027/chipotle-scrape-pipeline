# Chipotle hires Fernando Machado as chief brand officer

![A headshot of Fernando Machado](https://adage.com/resizer/v2/G7WAYUPJA5HYXGKW6K4L2L3CDM.jpg?smart=true&auth=16fcd812504a6e3434f48c47e28f203afb6e178ba61ce7a0393e54225af92574&width=3000&height=1687)

Fernando Machado is joining Chipotle in June. (Restaurant Brands International)

By Jon Springer

April 27, 2026 06:32 PM EDT

Fernando Machado, a decorated marketer best known for his work with Burger King, has been appointed chief brand officer at Chipotle Mexican Grill, effective June 1, the restaurant chain announced today. He will report to CEO Scott Boatwright and joins as Chipotle seeks to reverse a sales decline.
